## FAQ — 5 Real Q&As

**Q1: Is Credlens actually free?**
Yes. No credit card, no trial period. You enter your tools and spend, we show the audit on screen immediately. Email is only asked for if you want to save or share the report.

**Q2: How current is the pricing data?**
Every number traces back to an official vendor pricing page, verified at submission and cited in the report. If you spot something outdated, there's a "flag this" button on every line item.

**Q3: Do you store my spend data?**
Your tool selections and spend numbers are stored only to generate your shareable report URL. Company name and email are never shown in the public version.

**Q4: We use the API directly, not a plan. Can you still audit that?**
Yes. Enter your average monthly API spend for Anthropic, OpenAI, or Gemini directly. The audit benchmarks it against equivalent plan costs and flags if switching would save money at your usage level.

**Q5: What is Credex, and why is this free?**
Credlens is built by Credex a platform that sells discounted AI infrastructure credits (Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT) sourced from companies that overforecast. This tool helps you find the overspend; Credex helps you fix it. Genuine value first is the only distribution that works.
